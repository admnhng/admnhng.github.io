Introduction

Welcome to our PgMP Practice App. We are committed to protecting your privacy and want to be transparent about our non-collection of personal information.

Information We Don't Collect

Our app does not collect, store, or share any personal information about our users. We operate with complete user privacy in mind.

App Functionality

  • Translation feature works directly on-device.
  • No user accounts or registration required.
  • No analytics or tracking systems implemented.

Third-Party Services

We use Apple's App Store services solely for app distribution and in-app purchases. Any transaction data is handled directly by Apple, and we do not have access to your payment information.

Changes to Your Data

Since we don't collect any personal information, there is no user data to change, delete, or export.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Your Rights

  • Know that your privacy is protected.

GDPR Compliance

For European Union Users

1. Data Collection

- Our app does not collect or process any personal data as defined by GDPR.

- No personal information is transmitted to our servers or third parties.

- All app functionality works locally on your device.

2. Your GDPR Rights

While we don't collect personal data, you maintain your rights under GDPR including:

  • Right to be informed about data collection (which we confirm does not occur).
  • Right to access your data (not applicable as no data is collected).
  • Right to data portability (not applicable as no data is collected).
  • Right to be forgotten (not applicable as no data is collected).
3. Data Protection Officer (DPO)

Since we don't collect or process personal data, we are not required to appoint a DPO. However, for any privacy-related questions, please contact us.
